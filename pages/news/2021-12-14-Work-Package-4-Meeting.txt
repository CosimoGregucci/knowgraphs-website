# Automatic construction of metadata for knowledge graphs

In today's meeting, Maryam Mohammadi, PhD student at Maastricht university and ESR number 14 in KnowGraphs, presented her research on generating metadata for knowledge graphs in an automatic manner. During the presentation, she explained the problem and its significance, defined research questions, research hypothesis, related work and relation between FAIR principles and metadata. Finally, possible collaboration between ESRs discussed.

The representatives of Babelscape attended the meeting.
