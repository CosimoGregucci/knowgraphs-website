# Automatic construction of metadata for knowledge graphs

In today's meeting, Maryam Mohammadi, PhD student at Maastricht university and ESR number 14 in KnowGraphs, presented her research on generating metadata for knowledge graphs in an automatic manner. During the presentation, she explained the problem and its significance and she defined research questions as well as the research hypothesis. She also presented related work and the relation between FAIR principles and metadata. Finally, possible collaborations between ESRs were discussed.

The representatives of Babelscape attended the meeting.
